Kibbeh Nayeh

Ingredients

  • Raw lamb, beef, or veal
  • Fine bulgur wheat
  • Onion
  • Allspice
  • Lebanese seven spices
  • White pepper
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Drain the bulgar wheat. The bulgar should be soft when ready. Peel and quarter the onion.
  2. Split the meat into three batches. Ground the first two in a food processor and place them in a large bowl.
  3. Put the third batch in the food processor along with the raw onion and spices and process. Add this onion mixture to the rest of the ground meat.
  4. Add the bulgur to the meat and start kneading the mixture with your hands until everything is mixed together (it takes around 5-7 minutes so keep mixing). If you feel the mixture needs it, place your hands in a bowl of cold water and then mix again.
  5. Once everything is combined, shape the mixture into a large ball. Move it to the serving dish and start patting it down until it covers the entire serving plate.
  6. Use a fork to make lines across the kibbeh nayeh.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil. Decorate with greens or mint.
